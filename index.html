About project

In the new pandemic-affected reality, digital Turkish Medical Travel Exhibition (TMTEXPO) is an efficient tool to gather all health tourism related sectors from Turkey, Ukraine and CIS countries on one stage and reach out to potential patients.
TMTEXPO provides a platform for collaboration between Turkish hospitals, thermal resorts, spa&wellness centers and doctors, medical clinics, travel agencies, insurance companies, charity organizations, and potential patients from Ukraine and CIS.
During the expo, you will have a chance to build strong partnerships with medical institutions and doctors from Ukraine and CIS, share experiences regarding the latest technologies for diagnostics and treatment of patients in your clinic.
750+ exhibition visitors:
  • Clinics & Healthcare Workers
  • Medical Travel Companies
  • Potential Patients
  • Insurance Companies
  • Charity Organizations
  • Travel Agencies
About benefits

Benefits

Emin ÇAKMAK, Chairman of the Turkish Healthcare Travel Council (ТНТС)
“TMTEXPO provides excellent opportunities for developing cooperation between Ukraine, CIS countries and Turkey in the field of medical tourism. In 2019, Turkey treated 1.085.000 foreign patients with an average cheque of $9400. Ukraine and CIS countries is a priority market for us. Many Ukrainian and CIS tourists and healthcare tourists choose Turkey for recreation and treatment because of its unique combination of high quality service and affordable prices.”
As an exhibitor you will have a distinguished opportunity to:
  • create your online corporate stand
  • display brochures and other promotional materials about your clinic
  • participate in webinars with simultaneous translation
  • hold online meetings with potential partners
  • communicate with expo attendees in individual chats
Participation in the Expo is free of charge for all exhibitors.
