About project

In the new pandemic-affected reality, digital Turkish Medical Travel Exhibition (TMTEXPO)
is an efficient tool to gather all health tourism related sectors from Turkey, Ukraine and CIS countries on one stage and reach out to potential patients.
TMTEXPO provides a platform for collaboration between Turkish hospitals, thermal resorts, spa&wellness centers and doctors, medical clinics, travel agencies, insurance companies, charity organizations, and potential patients from Ukraine and CIS.
During the expo, you will have a chance to build strong partnerships with medical institutions and doctors from Ukraine and CIS, share experiences regarding the latest technologies for diagnostics and treatment of patients in your clinic.
750+ exhibition visitors:
  • Clinics & Healthcare Workers
  • Medical Travel Companies
  • Potential Patients
  • Insurance Companies
  • Charity Organizations
  • Travel Agencies
